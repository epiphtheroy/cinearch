### Introduction: The Breakthrough

Interpretations abound.

One might interpret the poet's repeated falls in the snow as a symbol of aging's inexorable decline.

Or perhaps seek superficial emotional bonds within the women's constant compliments.

However, the film's true center lies elsewhere.

The essential **Surface Enigma** is the hotel's isolated, snowy landscape and the characters' aimless wandering.

Its **Core Truth** reveals existential isolation as the film's unifying force, where mundane interactions mask profound disconnection.

This interpretation unravels the narrative by exposing how isolation permeates relationships, resolving enigmas like the poet's cryptic summons and the women's lingering heartbreaks.
